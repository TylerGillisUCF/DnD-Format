Generous heroes receive divine blessings. This encounter has NO FAIL STATE - only different outcomes based on choices made.]

WHEN THIS HAPPENS [DM announces to all players:] "After your seventh challenge, each of you continues along your separate paths. As you travel through different towns and villages, you each encounter a similar scene - though you don't know it's happening to all three of you. In each location, sitting by the roadside in tattered clothing, is a beggar asking passersby for help." [Run this encounter individually for Nick, Christopher, and Lena on their separate paths]

LOCATION DESCRIPTION [DM reads to the player:] "You're walking through a modest town when you notice someone sitting against a wall near the town square. [The Beggar speaks with a tired, humble voice:] "Excuse me, traveler... I'm sorry to trouble you, but... could you spare a few coins? I haven't eaten in two days. Anything would help. Even just one coin..." [The Beggar looks genuinely desperate but not pushy] "I know you're on an important journey. I can see it in your eyes. But if you could spare just a little..." [Trails off, looking down at their empty bowl]

> ⚠️ THE TRUE NATURE (HIDDEN FROM PLAYERS) [DM SECRET - DO NOT REVEAL YET:] This is not actually a beggar. This is a DISGUISED GOD testing the character of mortals. Gods sometimes walk among people in humble form to see who shows compassion without expectation of reward. The amount given matters LESS than the spirit in which it's given. Someone who gives 1 coin with genuine compassion may receive greater blessing than someone who gives 10 coins to show off or feel superior. The god is testing: Compassion - Do you care about those in need? Generosity - Are you willing to sacrifice for others? Humility - Do you help without needing recognition? Different responses lead to different blessings - but even walking away leads to a lesson, not a punishment.

PLAYER OPTIONS [DM to player:] "The beggar sits before you, asking for help. You currently have approximately [check their sheet] coins. What do you do? OPTION 1: Give generously (5+ coins) OPTION 2: Give modestly (2-4 coins) OPTION 3: Give minimally (1 coin) OPTION 4: Don't give coins but offer other help (food, advice, prayer) OPTION 5: Politely decline and walk away OPTION 6: Ignore them completely OPTION 7: Insult or mock them (WARNING: Very bad choice) What do you choose?"

[After the player walks away and is alone on the path:] [THE TRANSFORMATION] "As you continue down the path, you hear a voice behind you - the SAME voice, but different. Stronger. [The God speaks with warmth and authority:] "Forgive the deception, noble soul. [Smiles] "You gave GENEROUSLY, even when you yourself are on a difficult quest and need every resource. You saw someone suffering and chose to help without hesitation. That is TRUE virtue." PLUS: Divine Blessing effect (choose ONE): OPTION A - Combat Blessing: "Blessing of Divine Protection" - For the NEXT TWO encounters, +2 to all combat rolls OPTION B - Skill Blessing: "Blessing of Divine Wisdom" - For the NEXT TWO encounters, +2 to all non-combat rolls OPTION C - Fortune Blessing: "Blessing of Divine Fortune" - For the NEXT encounter, may reroll up to THREE failed rolls OPTION D - Ultimate Blessing: "Blessing of the Generous" - Once during the FINAL BOSS FIGHT, automatically turn any failed roll into a natural 20

> ⚠️ OUTCOME: Net coins: +5 (got refund PLUS extra!) Divine Blessing chosen (mark on character sheet!) Learned: Generosity is rewarded Feel: Validated in choosing kindness
